## Practical Fixes and Setup Guidance

If balances look wrong, review opening entries, fiscal year settings, and account mappings before changing posted documents. ERPNext Accounting software depends on consistent setup, so small configuration mistakes can affect reports later.

If ERPNext invoicing does not post as expected, check tax templates, item accounts, customer accounts, and company defaults. For ERPNext accounts payable issues, confirm supplier groups, expense accounts, payment terms, and purchase invoice status.

If reports feel incomplete, verify permissions and filters. ERPNext financial reports may depend on company, period, cost center, finance book, and currency selections. An ERPNext accounting tutorial can help new users understand how each filter affects the displayed totals.
